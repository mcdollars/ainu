name (AiNU)	parameter
B_rad	radiative recombination coefficient
C_Aug	Auger recombination coefficient
mun_ABS electron mobility of the semiconductor
mup_ABS hole mobility of the semiconductor
NA_ABS	doping concentration of the semiconductor
Nt	trap concentration
sigma_n	electron capture cross-section for trap-assisted recombination
sigma_p	hole capture cross-section for trap-assisted recombination (default: sigma_p=sigma_n)
ts_a	product of Nt and sigma_n (this parameter is used only when it is explicitly defined in Nt or sigma_n, e.g., when Nt=ts_a/sigma_n)
Et_r	trap energy level relative to the band edge (default:0.5, where 0=at the band edge; 0.5=mid-gap)
d	thickness of the semiconductor (default: 500[nm])
Epulse	TRPL laser injection level, i.e., pulse energy (default: 22[nJ/cm^2])
lam_ex	TRPL laser wavelength, i.e., excitation wavelength (default: 477[nm])
Eg_ABS	bandgap of the semiconductor (default: 1.6186[V], the unit of [V] will be changed automatically to [eV])
a0	optical absorption coefficient (default: 1e5[cm^-1])
